Frequently Asked Questions

Why did you name it Llama Carbon Copy?

The "Carbon Copy" portion comes from the idea that the software duplicates files. The "Llama" portion has to do with llamas being cute and hairy. We want our software to good for heavy lifting, while also being cute and hairy.

What's with the llamas? (aka What are those orange things?)

My wife and I have a healthy obsession with llamas. Secondly, because llamas are known for spitting and frolicking, we felt they were a natural mascot.

Originally, this product was called Spitting Image, which fits in nicely with llamas. That name was taken though and we had already fallen in love with the llamas so we searched for a name that was in the same spirit.

Does the Software work with (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Illustrator, Photoshop, etc.) ?

Yes, it works with most programs that are commercially available. Most programs write files to folders when you actively save them. Anytime a file is written to a folder you are watching it will be backed up.
