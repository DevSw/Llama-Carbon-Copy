Pricing

The pricing for Llama Carbon Copy is deliberately simple. It is $24.95 for a 1 user license.

A 1 user license entitles the purchaser to put Llama Carbon Copy on all computers owned by the purchaser. This includes laptops, secondary computers and any other computers the user owns.

This type of license includes prompt email support for the product.

Business Pricing

Businesses are welcome to purchase single user licenses in the amount required. If your business needs additional support, then a site license can be purchased for $249.95. A site license includes email support and one year of phone support.

Money Back guarantee

Should you find our product unsatisfactory for any reason, I offer an unconditional money back guarantee. I believe that if you aren't satisfied with my software, then I have not earned your money.

Software Updates

Software updates for the major version of the software you have purchased are free. Updates to the next major version are available at a reduced price. An example: If you have purchased version 1.1.2, and version 1.1.3 is released, then you are entitled to an upgrade. If you have purchased version 1.1.2 and version 1.2 is released, then you are entitled to purchase version 1.2 at a reduced price.
